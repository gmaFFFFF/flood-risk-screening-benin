# GIS in Flood Management

- **Monitoring:** Real-time data integration from sensors and weather feeds to track water levels and predict flood extents.
- **Response:** Using interactive maps and mobile apps to coordinate emergency services, identify at-risk populations, and manage evacuations.
- **Mitigation:** Analyzing historical data and terrain models to design infrastructure and implement land-use policies that reduce flood impact.
- **Planning:** Creating "what-if" scenarios and digital twins to simulate flood events, helping communities prepare long-term resilience strategies.
